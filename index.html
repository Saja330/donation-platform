About Us

🌿🌿🌿

(ATTAYAKUM) donation platform is a humanitarian initiative aimed at connecting generous donors with individuals and families in need. We believe that giving should be simple, transparent, and impactful.

Our platform helps hungry peoples by donating them meals. Main objective of our platform is to reduce hunger. Whether it's food, clothing, education support, or financial aid — every contribution matters.Because we know that food and clothes are needs.

Join us in making a real difference and spreading hope where it's needed most.

Donate Today
